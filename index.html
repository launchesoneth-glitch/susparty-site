Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I play SusParty? ▼
SusParty is a social deduction game where players are assigned roles - some are crew members trying to complete tasks, while others are imposters trying to sabotage the crew without getting caught. Use discussion and voting to find and eliminate the imposters!
How many players do I need? ▼
SusParty works best with 4-10 players, but you can play with as few as 4. The more players you have, the more exciting and unpredictable the game becomes!
Is SusParty free to play? ▼
Yes! SusParty is free to download and play. We offer an optional Premium upgrade that removes ads and unlocks exclusive features, but all core gameplay is completely free.
How do I restore my Premium purchase? ▼
If you've previously purchased Premium and need to restore it (for example, on a new device), go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Can I get a refund for my purchase? ▼
All purchases are processed through Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. Apple handles all refund requests for App Store purchases.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes! We take your privacy seriously. We only collect minimal data necessary for gameplay and analytics. We never sell your personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.
