Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

SusParty ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including game statistics and feature usage.
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases (handled securely by Apple).

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add features
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Send important updates about the app (if you opt in)

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Analytics: For app analytics and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (free version only). Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple: For processing in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Game data is stored locally on your device and, where applicable, synced securely through standard platform services.

Children's Privacy

SusParty is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Request a copy of your data

Advertising

The free version of SusParty displays advertisements through Google AdMob. You can remove all advertisements by purchasing SusParty Premium. Ads may be personalized based on your preferences; you can adjust ad personalization in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app or on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
